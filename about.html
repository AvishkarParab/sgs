Project Description


Title :- SMART GARBAGE SEGREGATION



Description:-

Developing this project has been a great experience. We have learned a diverse range of skills in different fields, such as IOT and programming languages such as C++. We also worked on the Arduino UNO. To obtain the final result we have had to work with many different technologies. The conveyor was successfully implemented. The metal was transported to metal while the non-metal was transported to the non-metal container using servo motor. All the circuitry was connected to PCBs and soldered. The Arduino received signal from sensor and through relays sent it to NodeMCU. From there the benefits were reflected on the website interface. We can conclude from this that the metal sorting system was successfully implemented.
